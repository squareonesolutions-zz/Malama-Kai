Equipment Lending Library

Malama Kai Foundation recognizes that budget cuts have left teachers with reduced access to resource materials and equipment for science teaching. We are designing a system so teachers can borrow Malama Kai Foundation environmental science equipment for free use in classrooms and on field trips. The resources include:

  • Water Quality Monitoring Equipment
    • Colorimeter 
    • pH/mV/Temp Meter
    • Conductivity/TDS Meter
    • Digital Titrator
    • Test Kits and Reagents (Dissolved Oxygen, Nitrogen, Phosphorus)
  • Classroom Science Materials and Equipment
    • Microscopes
    • Projector
    • Watershed Tabletop Model
    • Point Source Pollution Tabletop Model
    • Watershed Curricula, Grades K-12
  • Field Science Materials and Equipment
    • GPS units
    • Spotting Scope
    • Buckets and gloves
    • Field notebooks, composition books, clipboards
    • Plant presses
  • Camping and Outdoor Equipment
    • Tents, Sleeping mattresses
    • Snorkel gear
    • Camping stove, flashlights, lanterns
    • Large cooler
    • Water coolers
  • Informational Resources - Coastal and marine resource topics, various grade levels.
    • Books 
    • DVDs
    • Curriculum
    • Posters

Additionally, Malama Kai Foundation embodies a wide spectrum of expertise and information. Please contact us for assistance or insight in planning local watershed, marine, and coastal field trips and lessons, or to check out equipment from the Malama Kai Foundation Equipment Lending Library.
