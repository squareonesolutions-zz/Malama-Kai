Welcome to Malama Kai Foundation

Malama Kai Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to ocean stewardship for current and future generations through community service and public education. Founded in January 1991, Malama Kai Foundation raises funds and implements projects that help conserve Hawaiʻi’s coastal and marine resources, and educate people about these resources.

Education & Outreach

Public education and outreach are one of the principal purposes of the Malama Kai Foundation.

An educated public is necessary to sustain our coastal and marine resources in perpetuity.
  • Ocean Warriors
  • Reef Talk Lecture Series

Projects & Programs

Malama Kai Foundation is involved with a variety of different projects that further its mission and goals, ranging from education and outreach programs to conservation and protection programs of many kinds.
  • Day Use Moorings
  • Coastal Stewardship
